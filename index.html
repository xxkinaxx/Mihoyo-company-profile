about us

Founded in 2011, miHoYo creates products and experience that are out of the imagination. Beyond games like Houkai Gakuen2, Honkai Impact3, Tears if Themis, Genshin Impact, Honkai: Star Rail, miHoYo is also known for the dynamic desktop software N0va Desktop, the community product HoYolab, and related IP across animation, comic, music and merchandises. The upcoming Zenless Zone Zero is also widely followed by global gamers.

Under the misson of "Tech Otakus Save The World", miHoYo remains commited to technology research and development, exploring cutting-edge technology and developing industry-leading capabilities in the field of animation rendering, artificial intellignce, and cloud gaming.

Headquartered in Shanghai, China, miHoYo has 5.000 employees spread across global offices in Singapore, United States, Canada, Japan, and Korea.

Houkai Gakuen2

Houkai Gakuen 2 (Chinese: 崩坏学园2, translated: Honkai Academy 2), formerly known as HSoD The end of School, Zombiegal Kawaii and Guns Girl Z, a classic 2D side-scrolling shooter game from miHoYo (also known outside of China as HoYoverse).

On March 28, 2014, disaster called "Houkai" broke out in Chiba High School, Nagazora City. Kiana, who claims to be the "Valkyrie", embarked on her own destiny.

Honkai Impact 3rd

Honkai Impact 3rd (Chinese: 崩坏3; Japanese: 崩壊3rd) is an anime-styled, free-to-play RPG featuring combat that takes a lot of hints from popular series like Devil May Cry and Bayonetta. Players can string together various well-timed combos as well as activate powerful effects when dodging attacks with precision. Certain stages or events feature bullet hell, platforming, and even dungeon crawling elements. Players obtain items both through gameplay and gacha.

Tears Of Themis

In Tears Of Themis, miHoyo's detective adventure of love and wits, you are a new attorney at law on a journey to solve unusual cases together with your destined lover. As a great conspiracy begin to unfold, the line between good an evil blurs. Which way will your scales of justice tip?

Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact (Chinese: 原神 Yuánshén) is a free-to-play action role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo Co., Ltd. Outside of China, the publisher is miHoYo's subsidiary Cognosphere Pte., Ltd. d/b/a HoYoverse.

The game features a fantasy open-world environment and action based combat system using elemental magic, character switching, and a gacha monetization system for players to obtain new characters, weapons, and other resources. The game can only be played with an internet connection and features a limited multiplayer mode allowing up to four players in a world.

Honkai: Star Rail

A brand-new strategy game from miHoYo that adventures through the galaxy. You will explore new civilizations, encounter new friends, and embark on new adventures amongst myriads of fantastical world. Everything you seek can be found in the stars.
